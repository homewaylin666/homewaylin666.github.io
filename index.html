About

Hi — glad you found your way here.

I am a DPhil student at the Department of Biology, University of Oxford, and a member of the SalGo Team. At this stage of my journey, my ambition is to be able to call myself a cross-scale ecologist with full confidence by the time I finish my doctorate.

My research centres on integrating resilience from the individual level to the community level. To do this, I actively work across individual traits, population demography, and ecological interactions — from intraspecific density dependence to interspecific relationships that structure communities.

This site is my small museum: a collection of evidence for my academic evolution. Every entry here marks a step toward the ecologist I am adapting into.
